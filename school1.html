Selvagam Santhanalakshmi Noble School

Committed to Excellence in Primary & Middle School Education

Serving the Tirukalikundram, Chengalpattu community, we educate students from Grade 1 through Grade 9 with a focus on academic rigour, character development and holistic growth.

Tirukalikundram, Chengalpattu Grades 1–9

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing environment where each child discovers their strengths, develops critical thinking, and grows into a responsible citizen.

We combine a structured academic programme with co-curricular activities, personalised attention, and a secure campus to help children achieve their fullest potential.

Academic Approach

Our curriculum emphasises conceptual understanding, regular assessment, and skill development. Small class sizes allow teachers to tailor instruction and support individual learning needs.

Facilities

Classrooms: Well-lit, ventilated learning spaces.
Library: Age-appropriate books and reading corners.
Playground: Safe outdoor play and sports area.
Activity Rooms: Music, art and practical learning spaces.

Admissions

Admissions are open for Grades 1–9. Prospective parents may schedule a visit or submit an enquiry through our Contact page. Admissions details and forms are available on the Admissions page.
