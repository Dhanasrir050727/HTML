Grades 1 — 9

A supportive learning path from foundational years through middle school, focusing on academics, life skills and holistic growth.

Our Grade Structure

Grade 1

Building foundational literacy and numeracy with play-based learning.

Grade 2

Consolidating basics with introduction to science and environment.

Grade 3

Strengthening reading, writing and problem-solving skills.

Grade 4

Broader curriculum with project work and group activities.

Grade 5

Preparation for upper primary with assessments and enrichment.

Grade 6

Transition to middle school: deeper concepts and study skills.

Grade 7

Subject-oriented learning and practical lab activities.

Grade 8

Advanced competencies and critical thinking development.

Grade 9

Strong focus on concept clarity and preparation for board-level readiness.

Admissions & Curriculum

We follow a well-rounded syllabus with emphasis on values, arts, sports and technology. For admissions and detailed curriculum, please visit the Admissions page or contact us.
